# Quantum technology

Quantum technology is moving quickly across three fronts: computing, communication,
and sensing. Governments and companies are pouring money in, betting that quantum
machines will eventually crack problems classical computers cannot, that quantum links
will make communication far harder to intercept, and that quantum sensors will measure
the physical world with extraordinary precision. For now real-world deployments stay
limited, held back by hard engineering problems and the infrastructure still needed to
support them.
