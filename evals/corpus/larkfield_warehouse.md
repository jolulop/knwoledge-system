# Larkfield fulfillment — warehouse operations

## Cold storage zone

The cold storage zone at Larkfield holds perishable inventory between two and six degrees
Celsius. Pickers working this zone rotate out every forty minutes because of the cold, and
every pallet is scanned twice: once on intake and once again as it leaves for the loading
dock. Replenishment in cold storage is triggered automatically when a bin drops below twelve
units, and a supervisor signs off on each restock before it reaches the floor.

## Ambient storage zone

The ambient storage zone holds shelf-stable goods at ordinary room temperature. Pickers here
work standard two-hour shifts with no rotation, and each pallet is scanned only once, on
intake. Replenishment in ambient storage is triggered when a bin drops below forty units, and
restocks are batched overnight rather than signed off one at a time.
