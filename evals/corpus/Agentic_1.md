# Agentic AI — leadership brief

Across Northwind the first wave of AI assistants reached many teams but barely moved
our financial results: broad, shallow rollouts spread thin while the few deeper,
high-value efforts stalled before reaching production. The larger prize is agentic
AI — software agents that carry out multi-step work on their own — and capturing it
is mainly an organizational change, not a tooling one. We will need to redesign core
business processes, stand up clear governance over what agents may decide and touch,
rebuild parts of our enterprise architecture so agents can act safely, and sustain
direct sponsorship from the CEO to scale agents across the whole company rather than
in scattered pilots.
