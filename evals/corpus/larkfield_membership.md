# Larkfield membership tiers

## Standard tier

Standard members pay no annual fee and receive free shipping only on orders above fifty
dollars. They earn one reward point per dollar spent, and those points expire after twelve
months of inactivity. Standard members get early access to seasonal sales one day before the
general public, and support requests are answered within two business days.

## Premium tier

Premium members pay a ninety dollar annual fee and receive free shipping on every order with
no minimum. They earn three reward points per dollar spent, and those points never expire.
Premium members get early access to seasonal sales five days before the general public, and
support requests are answered within four hours by a dedicated concierge line.
