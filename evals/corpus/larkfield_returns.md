# Larkfield returns policy

## Domestic returns

Domestic customers may return any item within thirty days of delivery for a full refund.
Larkfield emails a prepaid shipping label at no charge, and refunds post back to the original
payment method within five business days of the warehouse receiving the item. Opened
electronics are still returnable domestically but carry a fifteen percent restocking fee.

## International returns

International customers have sixty days to start a return, but the outbound and return shipping
costs are never reimbursed. Refunds are issued as store credit rather than to the original
payment method, and that credit posts within fifteen business days. Opened electronics cannot
be returned at all on international orders, regardless of condition.
