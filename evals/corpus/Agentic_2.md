# Agentic AI — software delivery model

For engineering the opportunity is not just coding assistants but reworking how we
ship software: people and agents collaborating continuously, so delivery effectively
runs around the clock. That means standardizing our development workflow, removing the
manual handoffs between steps by passing machine-readable artifacts instead, and
feeding agents a knowledge graph of organizational context so they can run much of the
delivery lifecycle themselves while engineers concentrate on architecture and review.
Getting there means reshaping teams and reskilling engineers to supervise agents
rather than write every line; our early internal trials saw roughly three to four
times the throughput at a fraction of the manual effort.
