# Summary-first navigation

> [!summary]
> Summary-first navigation is the discipline of reading short page summaries before opening full page bodies. It keeps agent navigation efficient by making the index and callouts act as a lightweight routing layer.

## Definition

Summary-first navigation means every important page has a concise summary designed for future humans and agents deciding whether to open the full page.

## Why It Matters

Without a summary layer, agents tend to open too many files and query cost grows quickly as the corpus grows.
